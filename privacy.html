Matryoshka — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Matryoshka is a Telegram Mini App game. We collect the minimum data needed to operate the game and process payments.

What we collect

  • Telegram user data — your Telegram ID, first name, username, language code. Provided by Telegram only after you open the Mini App.
  • Gameplay state — score, high score, streak, in-game currency, cosmetic unlocks, daily-claim history. Stored in your browser's localStorage and on our server keyed by your Telegram ID.
  • Purchase records — Telegram Stars purchases are processed by Telegram. We store a record of which SKUs you bought so we can restore them.

What we do NOT collect

  • No ads, no ad networks, no ad tracking.
  • No third-party analytics outside of optional Mixpanel event logging if enabled by the operator.
  • No payment card data — all payments are processed by Telegram in Stars.

Data retention

Game state and purchase history are retained while your account is active. You can request deletion by messaging the bot or contacting the operator.

Contact

Questions? Message the bot from inside Telegram.
